## 🧠 Overview

**Escape the Backrooms** is a cooperative psychological horror game inspired by the unsettling concept of endless liminal spaces and distorted reality. Players must navigate mysterious environments filled with hidden dangers, strange entities, and constantly shifting corridors while searching for a way to escape.

The game emphasizes atmospheric tension, exploration, and teamwork as players move through a series of eerie levels inspired by internet horror mythology. Each area introduces unique visual themes, environmental hazards, and unpredictable threats that force players to remain alert and coordinated at all times.

Survival depends heavily on communication and careful observation. Players must solve environmental puzzles, manage limited resources, avoid hostile entities, and uncover safe routes through maze-like structures where danger can emerge unexpectedly from the darkness.

Escape the Backrooms creates a constant feeling of unease through immersive lighting, ambient sound design, and isolated environments that blur the line between familiarity and fear. The combination of psychological horror and cooperative gameplay results in highly suspenseful multiplayer sessions.

The game supports both solo and online cooperative play, allowing groups of players to experience exploration and survival together while facing terrifying encounters inside increasingly surreal environments. Every level introduces new mechanics and challenges that keep progression unpredictable and engaging.

Whether escaping hostile creatures, exploring abandoned corridors, or surviving bizarre liminal spaces with friends, **Escape the Backrooms** delivers an immersive horror experience for Windows players seeking atmospheric cooperative gameplay.
